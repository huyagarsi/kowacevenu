Haylie Duff News - Us Weekly

Name:Haylie Duff

Hometown:Houston, TX

Birthday:February 19, 1985

About

Haylie Duff rose to fame after appearing on the WB show 7th Heaven from 2005 to 2007. The Texas native, who is the older sister of Hilary Duff, continued to blossom on screen as Summer Wheatly in 2004’s Napoleon Dynamite.

The actress then starred in the 2006’s romantic comedy Material Girls alongside sister Hilary and lent her voice to the animated flick Foodfight! Haylie has since appeared in Badge of Honor, The Sandman and Hacker.

Also a talented vocalist, she contributed songs to the A Cinderella Story soundtrack, singing on the hit single “Our Lips are Sealed” and “One In This World.” The star’s other soundtrack credits include The Lizzie McGuire Movie and Stuck in the Suburbs.

She started her own cooking blog called the Real Girl’s Kitchen in 2012. Its success led to the Real Girl’s Kitchen cooking show — which ran for two seasons from 2014 to 2015 — and a cookbook by the same name.

In 2017, Haylie launched the children’s clothing brand Little Moon Society. She has been dating Matt Rosenberg since 2012. The couple got engaged in April 2014 and share two children, daughter Lulu and son Ryan.
