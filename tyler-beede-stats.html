Know About Tyler Beede; Stats, Salary, Wife, Height, Net Worth, College

Very rare are players who snub, MLB team to play for their college, and Tyler Beede is one of them. The 26-year-old has already proven his worth at the baseball pitch with a great admiring wife that he has.

American professional baseball pitcher, Tyler Beede plays for MLB team, San Francisco Giants. The player who wears jersey number 34 was selected in the 2014 draft whose debut came in 2018.

Tyler Beede Bio & Family

The player was born as Tyler Joseph Beede on 23 May 1993 in Worcester, Massachusetts to parents, Walter and Cheryl Beede. Apart from his father and mother, he has an older brother named Kyle. Tyler went to Lawrence Academy at Groton, Massachusetts to complete his education and play high school baseball.

Tyler stands at a height of 6 feet 4 inches (1.91 m) tall and weighs around 96 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The MLB star career commenced with his college team, Vanderbilt Commodores. During his 2012 season, his stats were 1–5 win-loss including 4.52 earned run average (ERA) and 68 strikeouts. Likewise, in his 2013 sophomore season, he won his first 14 starts and ended the season with 14–1 where his stats were 2.32 ERA and 103 strikeouts.

Tyler Beede MLB stats (Picture: MLB)

After college, the Giants picked him in the first round of the 14th overall in the 2014 MLB draft. From Arizona Giants to the Salem-Keizer Volcanoes and San Jose Giants, he got the fortune to play at the Class A-Advanced California League. From then he got promoted to the Richmond Flying Squirrels of the Class AA Eastern League in June.

Almost four years later, The Giants promoted Beede to the major leagues on 10 April 2018. As if for now, his salary playing for Giants is $555,000 which makes his net worth to be $3 million.

Who Is Tyler Beede’s Wife?

The shinning MLB star enjoys over a year of beautiful matrimony with wife, Allie DeBerry. She was his longtime girlfriend who went to exchange wedding vows on 26 November 2017. Tyler even celebrated their first marriage anniversary sharing an IG post which states,

Some Interesting Facts About Tyler Beede

  • Tyler chose to play for his college team, Vanderbilt Commodores after MLB team, Toronto Blues picked him in the 2011 MLB Draft.
  • During his college career, he was named to the All-Southeastern Conference freshman team.
  • He was one of the finalists for the Golden Spikes Award and Dick Howser Trophy in his sophomore season.
  • Tyler also got the fortune to be in the playing squad of the United States collegiate national team during the summer.
  • In 2014, his stats were 4.04 ERA and 116 strikeouts which helped his college Vanderbilt to secure the College World Series.
