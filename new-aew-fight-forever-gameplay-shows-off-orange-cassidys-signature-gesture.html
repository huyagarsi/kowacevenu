New AEW Fight Forever Gameplay Shows Off Orange Cassidys Signature Gesture

Orange Cassidy has worked hard to establish himself as one of the most beloved stars in all of AEW. His cool character has helped him gain fanfare in AEW. He is part of AEW Fight Forever and now new gameplay has revealed him performing his signature gesture.

‘AEW: Fight Forever’ had been hit with a number of delays since its announcement in November 2020. This has made fans believe the game is legitimately cursed and that it would never see the light of day.

Of course, their fears were finally dispelled as it was officially announced that AEW: Fight Forever will be releasing on June 29th on all modern consoles as well as on PC. Of course, fans are extremely excited for its release.

As revealed by new gameplay, Orange Cassidy could be seen performing his signature hands in pockets gesture while wrestling MJF in the game. Naturally, fans loved the authenticity and attention to detail with the move.

AEW Fight Forever will also feature ton of blood, so violence is a guarantee in the game. We will have to see how AEW Fight Forever is received by gamers, as the company’s first venture into the world of video games seems to be a promising one.
