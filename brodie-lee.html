Brodie Lee aka Luke Harper (born December 16, 1979 – died December 26, 2020) was an American professional wrestler and actor from Rochester, New York. Many people know him for being for working with various circuits such as Squared Circle Wrestling, Ring of Honor, Dragon Gate USA, etc under the name of Luke.

He was one of the most celebrated wrestlers from 2003 to 2012 and earned a lot of success in his career. Furthermore, he also participated in Florida Championship Wrestling, Dragon Gate, etc. He then introduced by NXT and became a member of The Wyatt Family alongside Erick Rowan and Bray Wyatt.

They won the WWE SmackDown Tag Team Championship as well as NXT Tag Team Championship. In 2014, Brodie got the honor of becoming a one-time WWE Intercontinental Champion. His AEW debut was made in March of 2020.

On December 26, 2020, very devasting news was published that Luke Harper died at the age of 41 after fighting a long battle with lung problems. He passed away in hospital.
