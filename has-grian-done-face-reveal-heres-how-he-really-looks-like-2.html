Has Grian Done Face Reveal? Heres How He Really Looks Like

The English Youtuber who goes by the online moniker Grian makes Minecraft building tutorials and is part of the popular Hermitcraft server.

Hermitcraft is a whitelisted Minecraft community founded by Generikb on April 12, 2012. The server has continually grown with many vanillas (unmodded) and modded seasons.

There have been nine seasons so far, and the server is currently administered by Xisumavoid, also one of the earliest members of the server.

Members of the Hermitcraft server are called Hermits. To be a Hermit, one must be invited as it is an invite-only server.

As it stands, Hermits are usually popular Minecraft-related content creators who post Youtube videos or stream on Twitch about the server. Currently, there are 26 whitelisted Hermits on the server, among which 23 are active and three are inactive.

Even the person with the lowest subscriber count on the server has subscribers in the hundreds of thousands. Grian has the second-highest highest subscriber count amongst all the servers.

Besides Hermitcraft, the Youtuber is known for his affiliation with the immensely popular Minecraft-based MMORPG called WynnCraft, which he helped create.

Besides Minecraft-related content, Grian also makes vlogs about his holiday travels, invitations to Minecraft conventions, and occasional non-Minecraft games.

The Youtuber has amassed 7.89 million subscribers on his main channel. He also has a second channel called Two Much Grian, with 703K subscribers.

Has Grian Done A Face Reveal?

English Minecraft Youtuber Grian has done a face reveal in a short video with the Youtuber Mumbo.

Mumbo, or Oliver “Oli” Brotherhood, is a fellow Minecraft Youtuber and a member of the Hermitcraft server. He goes by the online moniker Mumbo Jumbo and has a channel with 8.54 million subscribers.

However, outside his main channel, the Youtuber has a second channel, Mumbo, with 624K subscribers. The Youtuber focuses on filmmaking, videography, and documentaries on this channel.

On June 24, 2018, the Mumbo channel released a short five minutes and forty-five-second video entitled Grian: The Cat Whisperer.

The video was about Grian and his cats. It was the first time his fans had seen the Youtuber’s face.

Upon the reveal of his face, many fans were surprised, but even more so, they were divided amongst people who thought Grian looked exactly like they expected and those who thought he looked entirely different.

Many fans noted how he looked like his Youtube icon. Since the reveal, Grian has used his face as his Twitter and Instagram profile picture.

The latter features a picture of him and his wife.

What Is Grian’s Real Name?

English Minecraft Youtuber Grian has revealed his full name to be Charles Batchelor.

Batchelor resides in England but has not revealed what part as of yet. Batchelor is very private regarding his wife and has not revealed his wife’s name on his social media.

However, he mentioned that they married on August 15, 2020. The Youtuber has stated that they went on their honeymoon before their wedding due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Batchelor has two cats named Maui and Pearl. He has mentioned that his cats, alongside his wife, help with his severe anxiety.

The Youtuber has a degree in biology but has not mentioned where he went to University. He also has two siblings, a brother, and a sister.

He is five feet and six inches and loves the color brown. This past July and August, the Youtuber went to Ghana for two weeks to supervise a built school he helped fund.
