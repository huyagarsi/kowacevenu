Pearl Thusi celebrates her daughter Thando's 13th birthday in style

- Pearl Thusi's eldest daughter Thando Mokoena turned 13 last week and she shared pictures from her "slumber party" inspired event

- Pearl spoke about how much she appreciated her daughter and described her as her treasure and gift from God

- Thando received many beautiful gifts, including a surprising present from Black Coffee

Media personality Pearl Thusi celebrated her daughter Thando Mokoena's 13th birthday in absolute style. Thando's birthday party had a classic "slumber party" theme and if the pictures she shared were anything to go by, it was a very good time for everyone involved.

Thando's birthday was last Saturday and Pearl had only wonderful things to say about her eldest daughter. In a touching post on social media, Pearl spoke about how much she appreciates her daughter. She went on about how her daughter was her treasure and a gift from God.

Pearl wrote: “I have so much to say about how proud I am of you, your loving nature, your big heart, you’re the better version of me.”

The birthday celebration was hosted in a rented mansion and the guest list was filled with many local celebrities. Pearl's best friend DJ Zinhle was also in attendance and brought along her daughter Kairo Forbes.

Pearl went all out with the decor which was inspired by Thando's favourite colour blue. She also made use of black-owned businesses when it came to the food and the sleepwear which was supplied to the guests.

It was definitely a day to remember as Thando took to social media to share her favourite moments from the party. She also shared her favourite gift from the day which was skateboard from Black Coffee.
