LIL KIM AND MR. PAPERS CELEBRATE DAUGHTERS 6TH BIRTHDAY

Lil Kim’s daughter, Royal Reign, is officially 6-years-old! The youngster’s parents took to Instagram in celebration of her special day.

Lil Kim shared a clip from Royal’s birthday last year to shed light on how fast the time has passed. “This was my baby’s birthday last year when she turned 5 [years old],” the celebrity mom shared with her social media fans. “It feels just like yesterday,” Lil Kim reflected. “Happy Birthday Princess.”

Mr. Papers also took to Instagram to commemorate his daughter’s special day. The celebrity father shared clips from his little one’s birthday celebration, which showed a much smaller scale of festivities because of the coronavirus pandemic.

“This was the best day of my life,” Mr. Papers said of his daughter’s birthday celebration. “Happy Birthday baby,” the celebrity father said.

Royal Reign is Lil Kim and Mr. Papers’ only child together. The celebrity father cleared the air about his relationship with both his child’s mother one year after their daughter’s birth.

“The truth is , there is no female out here , rapper chick /celeb chick or regular female that is Badder or flyer than Kim,” Mr. Papers shared. “She is an amazingly beautiful girl inside & out and the realest girl I kno … My heart is with her,” the celebrity dad said.
