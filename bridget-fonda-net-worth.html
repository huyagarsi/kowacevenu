FAQs about Bridget Fonda

Who is Bridget Fonda?

She is a retired American actress, best known for her roles in movies like The Godfather Part III, Single White Female, and Point of No Return.

How old is Bridget Fonda?

As of 2024, she is 60 years old. She was born on January 27, 1964, in Los Angeles, California.

Why did she retire from acting?

She retired from acting in 2002 after a car accident that resulted in a fractured vertebra. She decided to focus on her family life after marrying composer Danny Elfman in 2003.

Bridget comes from a prominent acting family. Her father is Peter Fonda, her grandfather is Henry Fonda, and her aunt is Jane Fonda. She also has a brother named Justin Fonda.
