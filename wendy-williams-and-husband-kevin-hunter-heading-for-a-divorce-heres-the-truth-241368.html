Wendy Williams And Husband Kevin Hunter Heading For A Divorce? Heres The Truth!

As fans know, rumors have been going around that the talk show host and her husband Kevin Hunter are getting close to a divorce. But is it really true or not?

One insider close to Wendy Williams and her hubby chatted with HollywoodLife, telling the outlet if they are truly separating or not.

‘There is no truth at all to the separation rumors swirling around Wendy and her husband Kevin. Wendy and Kevin have been together a very long time, they have longevity on their side with over 22 years married. Like any other couple they have had their ups and downs, but they are happy and as strong and solid as ever,’ she source dished.

Meanwhile, Williams has been absent from her TV show ever since December 20 after suffering a shoulder fracture.

Later on, it was announced that her break had been extended indefinitely due to some complications of her Graves disease as well!

Wendy has been dealing with her health problems and now, she is also being stressed out by the speculations that her decades-long marriage might be ending soon.

This is not the first time the rumors come into the forefront as they have been circulating quite a lot lately.

The insider tells the site that the pair hasn’t appreciated the rumors about the two of them ending things.

‘They do not like the split rumors and feel they are nonsense. They have not only a good personal relationship, but a strong business relationship too [Kevin is also Wendy’s manager].
