Media Artist Kit Porter Passes Away at 40

The art world is mourning the loss of one of its brightest talents, Kit Porter, who passed away on December 23, 2023, following a courageous battle with breast cancer.

Kit Porter: A Life Dedicated to Art

Born Katharine Lea Coleman on March 21, 1983, in Wilmington, North Carolina, Porter was known for her innovative and captivating mixed media artwork. Her work was admired for its depth, creativity, and the unique perspective it brought to the viewer.

A Battle Bravely Fought

Porter’s life took an unexpected turn when she was diagnosed with breast cancer. Despite the challenges she faced, she remained resilient, demonstrating remarkable courage and strength throughout her battle.

Remembering Kit Porter

Porter’s untimely demise has left her family, friends, and the entire art community in mourning. She will be remembered not only for her significant contributions to the field of mixed media art but also for her resilience, courage, and unwavering spirit.

The Legacy of Kit Porter

As we remember Porter, we also celebrate her legacy that will continue to inspire upcoming artists. Her work will continue to live on, reminding us of her talent, her passion, and her indomitable spirit.

In conclusion, Kit Porter’s passing is a significant loss to the world of art. Her talent, courage, and resilience will be remembered fondly by all who knew her and were touched by her work.
