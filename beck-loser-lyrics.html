Beck Loser Lyrics

Loser is a captivating song by American singer-songwriter Beck, released in 1994 as the lead single from his debut studio album, “Mellow Gold.”

This alternative rock anthem quickly became an unexpected hit, propelling Beck into the mainstream music scene.

With its eclectic blend of genres, including hip-hop, folk, and punk, “Loser” showcases Beck’s unique musical style and witty lyrics, making it a defining track of the 1990s alternative rock movement.
