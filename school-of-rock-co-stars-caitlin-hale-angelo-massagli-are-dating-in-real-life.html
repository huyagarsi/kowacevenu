'School of Rock' Co-Stars Caitlin Hale &amp; Angelo Massagli Are Dating in Real Life!

School of Rock fans were hit with a major revelation over the weekend.

Two of the movie’s stars – Caitlin Hale and Angelo Massagli – are dating!

The two former child stars portrayed classmates Marta and Frankie in the 2003 comedy that also starred Jack Black, and the news of them being a couple went viral after a TikTok user pointed out that the duo had been dating for over three years.

The TikTok users post has since racked up over 227,000 likes and 2,700 comments.

In a funny caption about their relationship, Caitlin shared a cute new selfie of the two, confirming the rumors were true.

Caitlin and Angelo have mostly moved on from acting. She’s now an OB-GYN ultrasound technologist and he graduated from the University of Miami School of Law in 2019.

“Who would’ve thought a law degree would be the second best thing I left MIA with. On to NYC ?,” he shared on his Instagram feed after his graduation.

Most recently, Caitlin shared an image of Angelo by her side in the hospital after she underwent endometriosis surgery last month.

“It’s been 4 weeks since my second laparoscopic endometriosis excision surgery,” she wrote, thanking her medical team and nothing that she “couldn’t have done it without @angelo_massagli waiting on me hand and foot during my two weeks of bed rest!”
