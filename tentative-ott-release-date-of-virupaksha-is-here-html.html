Tentative OTT release date of Virupaksha is here

Published on May 15, 2023 7:00 pm IST

Sai Dharam Tej and Samyuktha starrer blockbuster film, Virupaksha, is still performing well at the box office in Telugu states. The mystical thriller, directed by Karthik Varma Dandu, is now gearing up for an OTT release.

According to rumors, the film will be premiered on Netflix on May 21, 2023. However, we should wait for an official announcement from the OTT platform.

Virupaksha also features Rajiv Kanakala, Sunil, Soniya Singh, Abhinav Gomatam, Shyamala, Brahmaji, and others in significant roles. The music director of the film is Ajaneesh Loknath, and it is produced under the SVCC banner.
