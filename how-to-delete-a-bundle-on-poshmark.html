How to delete a bundle on poshmark

How do I remove an item from bundle?

Open the Bundle in the tree or in the list view. The Bundle shows its contents in the list view on the right, and a Bundle tab appears in the Ribbon toolbar. Select the item or items you want to remove in the list and select Remove from Bundle in the Bundle tab. The selected items are removed from the Bundle.

How do I cancel a bundle offer on poshmark as a seller?

On the web
  • Select your profile picture at the top-right of the page.
  • Select My Offers.
  • Select the offer.
  • Select Help.
  • From the drop-down menu, select Cancel Offer.

    • Why can’t you delete poshmark listings?

    Poshmark does not remove sold listings at this time, even if the order has been cancelled since sold listings act as a record of previous transactions.

    Are bundle comments private on poshmark?

    Should I delete my sold items on poshmark?

    Poshmark does not remove sold listings at this time, even if the order has been cancelled since sold listings act as a record of previous transactions. Once a listing is sold, a user will not be able to purchase it again.

    What happens if poshmark buyer does not accept?

    Poshmark protects sellers
