Joe Francis' Girlfriend Abbey Wilson Pregnant With Twin Girls!

Going wild with babies! Joe Francis and his longtime girlfriend Abbey Wilson are expecting twin girls, the Girls Gone Wild creator, 40, tells Us Weekly exclusively. 

"We both wanted girls and we wanted them to be healthy and free of genetic diseases so we chose to do IVF," Wilson, 26, told Us. "I have always heard women say you just know when you are pregnant and I totally understand that now.  Your body, smell, sensitivity and cravings all change and mine did from day one. I found out shortly after a blood test confirmed the results and called Joe immediately, we were both screaming and so loud with excitement from each end of the phone!"

Francis isn't concerned about being the father of two girls. "We chose to have girls," he said. "I believe people will finally understand my love, respect and admiration for women. I love girls."

The couple has happily been telling their friends the big news, especially the ones who are parents themselves.

"Kourtney [Kardashian] and Scott [Disick] were the first in the Kardashian family we told and they have already given us so much amazing advice," Francis told Us. 

Wilson adds, "Mario and Courtney Lopez took us baby shopping at Bel Bambini, and Mario is now coaching Joe on fatherhood!"
