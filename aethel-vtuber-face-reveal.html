Virtual Youtuber

A VTuber is a digital performer who makes a virtual avatar by using computer graphics and real-time motion control software or hardware.

Most VTubers are English- and Japanese-speaking YouTubers or live streamers who use avatar designs. This is a digital trend that started in Japan in the middle of the 2010s and has spread around the world since then.
