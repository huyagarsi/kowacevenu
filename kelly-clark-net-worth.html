What is Kelly Clark's Net Worth?

Kelly Clark is an American snowboarder who has a net worth of $1.5 million dollars. Kelly Clark was born in Dover, Vermont in July 1983. Clark won a Gold medal in Halfpipe at the 2002 Salt Lake City Olympic Games. She started to snowboard at seven years old and started competing in 1999 before becoming a member of the US Snowboard team in 2000. She won Bronze medals in Halfpipe at the 2010 Vancouver and 2014 Sochi Olympic games. Clark has also won Gold medals in Superpipe at the Aspen Winter X Games in 2006, 2011, 2012, 2013, and 2014.

She won Silver medals in the same event in 2004, 2009, 2010, and 2015, and a Bronze in 2008. She also won a Gold medal at the Winter Dew Tour 2008-2009 Dew Cup in Superpipe and Silver medals at the Winter Dew Tour 2009 Breckenridge, 2009 Mt. Snow, and 2009 Northstar-at-Tahoe. In 2015 Kelly was awarded the ESPY for Best Female Action Sports Athlete. Kelly is a member of the 2018 Olympic team that competed in South Korea.
