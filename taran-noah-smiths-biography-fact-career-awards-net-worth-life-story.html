Taran Noah Smiths biography, fact, career, awards, net worth &amp; life story

  • Height: 6′ (1.83 m)
  • Marital Status: Divorced (Heidi van Pelt )
  • Full Name: Taran Noah Smith
  • Nationality: American
  • Date of Birth: April 8, 1984
  • Occupation: Actor
  • Education: University of Southern California

Best known as Marcus “Mark” Jason Taylor in the hit sitcom titled Home Improvement, Taran Noah Smith is an American actor with an estimated net worth of $300 thousand.

Smith started working as an actor for Home Improvement at the age of 7 and by the time the show ended, he was already 16 years old. At the age of 17, he married Heidi Van Pelt who was 16 years his senior. After 6 years, the couple divorced.

Among Smith’s most notable projects include ABC TGIF, Ebbie, 7th Heaven, Batman Beyond and Little Bigfoot 2: The Journey Home. For playing a role in Home Improvement,  Taran Noah Smith has earned two nominations for Young Artist Award, for Best Performance in a TV Drama or Comedy Series – Leading Young Actor and Outstanding Actor Under Ten in a Television Series categories. He also won two Young Artist Award for Exceptional Performance by a Young Actor Under Ten and Outstanding Youth Ensemble in a Television Series categories.
